Degree Programs Offered

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (B.Sc. CS)

Duration: 3 Years

Eligibility: High school diploma or equivalent with a focus on mathematics and science.

Description: The B.Sc. in Computer Science is designed to give students a solid foundation in computing, programming, algorithms, and software development. Graduates are prepared for careers in software engineering, data science, and more.

Bachelor of Arts in English Literature (B.A. English)

Duration: 3 Years

Eligibility: High school diploma or equivalent with a focus on humanities and language.

Description: This program explores classic and contemporary works of English literature, equipping students with critical thinking, writing, and analytical skills. Graduates can pursue careers in teaching, writing, and publishing.

Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)

Duration: 3 Years

Eligibility: High school diploma or equivalent with a focus on commerce or related subjects.

Description: The BBA program covers essential topics in business management, marketing, finance, and entrepreneurship. Graduates are prepared for leadership roles in the corporate world or to start their own business ventures.

Bachelor of Science in Nursing (B.Sc. Nursing)

Duration: 4 Years

Eligibility: High school diploma or equivalent with a focus on biology and health sciences.

Description: The B.Sc. in Nursing prepares students for a career in healthcare, providing them with knowledge in patient care, medical treatments, and healthcare administration. Graduates can work in hospitals, clinics, or pursue advanced medical studies.

Master of Business Administration (MBA)

Duration: 2 Years

Eligibility: Bachelor's degree in any field, preferably with work experience in business or management.

Description: The MBA is an advanced degree aimed at professionals looking to enhance their business leadership skills. The program covers advanced topics in management, finance, operations, and strategic decision-making.

Master of Computer Applications (MCA)

Duration: 2 Years

Eligibility: Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, IT, or related fields. For lateral entry, a BCA or equivalent degree is required.

Description: The MCA program is designed to provide advanced knowledge in computer science and applications, including software development, database management, and IT project management. Graduates are equipped to pursue careers as software developers, system analysts, IT consultants, and more.
